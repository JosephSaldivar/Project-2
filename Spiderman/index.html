Spider-Man (1)

Origin

Spider-man first appeared in August 1962 in Amazing Fantasy #15 by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby. They intruduced us to Peter Parker, a teenage boy that lives with his aunt and uncle. Peter loves chemistry so he went to a science fair and on that given day, he was bitten by a radoactive spider. That day would change his life forever. His Uncle Ben was shot and killed and Peter blames himself for the death of his uncle because he could have stopped the thief. From that day forward Peter Parker would become Spider- Man and follow his Uncle Ben's "With Great Power, Comes Great Responsiblity".
